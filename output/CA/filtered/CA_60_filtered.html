Harmonized System classification Product specific rule for sufficient production pursuant to Article 5
Chapter 60Knitted or crocheted fabrics
60.01-60.06 Spinning of natural or man-made staple fibres or extrusion of man-made filament yarn, in each case accompanied by knitting; Knitting, accompanied by dyeing, flocking or coating; Flocking, accompanied by dyeing or printing; Dyeing of yarn of natural fibres accompanied by knitting; or Twisting or texturing, accompanied by knitting provided that the value of the non-twisted or non-textured yarns used does not exceed 47.5 per cent of the transaction value or ex-works price of the product.
