Harmonized System classification Product specific rule for sufficient production pursuant to Article 5
Chapter 39Plastics and articles thereof
39.01-39.15 A change from any other heading; or A change from within any one of these headings, whether or not there is also a change from any other heading, provided that the value of non-originating materials classified in the same heading as the final product does not exceed 50 per cent of the transaction value or ex-works price of the product; or A change from within any one of these headings, whether or not there is also a change from any other heading, provided that the net weight of non-originating material classified in the same heading as the final product does not exceed 50 per cent of the net weight of the product.
39.16-39.26 A change from any other heading.
