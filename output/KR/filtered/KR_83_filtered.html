8302 41 Other mountings, fittings and similar articles suitable for buildings Manufacture from materials of any heading, except that of the product. However, other materials of heading 8302 may be used, provided that their total value does not exceed 20% of the ex-works price of the product

8306 21 to 8306 29 Statuettes and other ornaments, of base metal Manufacture from materials of any heading, except that of the product. However, other materials of heading 8306 may be used, provided that their total value does not exceed 30% of the ex-works price of the product
