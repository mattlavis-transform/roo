HS heading Description of product Working or processing, carried out on non-originating materials, which confers originating status
Chapter 8
Edible fruit and nuts; peel of citrus fruits or melons
Manufacture in which:
  • all the fruit, nuts and peels of citrus fruits or melons of Chapter 8 used are wholly obtained, and
  • the weight of sugar[2] used does not exceed 40 % of the weight of the final product
