HS heading Description of product Working or processing, carried out on non-originating materials, which confers originating status
Chapter 19
Preparations of cereals, flour, starch or milk; pastrycooks’ products
Manufacture from materials of any heading, except that of the product, in which:
  • the weight of the materials of Chapter s 2, 3 and 16 used does not exceed 20 % of the weight of the final product, and
  • the weight of the materials of headings 1006 and 1101 to 1108 used does not exceed 20 % of the weight of the final product, and
  • the individual weight of sugar[8] and of the materials of Chapter 4 used does not exceed 40 % of the weight of the final product, and
  • the total combined weight of sugar[9] and the materials of Chapter 4 used does not exceed 60 % of the weight of final product
[8] See Introductory Note 4.2.
