Chapter 18
Cocoa and cocoa preparations
Manufacture from materials of any heading, except that of the product, in which
  • the individual weight of sugar[6] and of the materials of Chapter 4 used does not exceed 40 % of the weight of the final product, and
  • the total combined weight of sugar[7] and the materials of Chapter 4 used does not exceed 60 % of the weight of final product
