HS heading Description of product Working or processing, carried out on non-originating materials, which confers originating status
Not exceeding 50 cc
Manufacture:
  • in which the value of all the materials used does not exceed 40 % of the ex–works price of the product;
  • where the value of all the non–originating materials used does not exceed the value of the originating materials used
Manufacture in which the value of all the materials used does not exceed 20 % of the ex–works price of the product

Exceeding 50 cc
Manufacture:
  • in which the value of all the materials used does not exceed 40 % of the ex–works price of the product;
  • where the value of all the non–originating materials used does not exceed the value of the originating materials used
Manufacture in which the value of all the materials used does not exceed 25 % of the ex–works price of the product

Other
Manufacture:
  • in which the value of all the materials used does not exceed 40 % of the ex–works price of the product;
  • where the value of all the non–originating materials used does not exceed the value of the originating materials used
Manufacture in which the value of all the materials used does not exceed 30 % of the ex–works price of the product
