Chapter 94 Furniture; bedding, mattresses, mattress supports, cushions and similar stuffed furnishings; lamps and lighting fittings, not elsewhere specified or included; illuminated signs, illuminated name-plates and the like; prefabricated buildings Manufacture from materials of any heading, except that of the product
or
Manufacture in which the value of all the materials used does not exceed 70% of the ex-works price of the product
